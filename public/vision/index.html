FAQ

Common questions

Do I really own the IP?

+

Yes — 100%. Every agent, piece of software or content you build on TotallyWild.ai belongs to you. We have no claim over your output, ever.

How does the 80% revenue share work?

+

In your first year on the Pro plan, you keep 80 cents of every dollar earned on the Marketplace. After year one it steps down slightly — but you always keep the majority.

Can I use my own AI models?

+

Absolutely. You can bring your own models, fine-tune ours, or use our default stack — all within the Studio builder.

Is there a free plan?

+

Yes — the Explorer plan is forever free with 3 AI agent builds per month and 1 Marketplace listing. The Creator plan starts at A$39/month with more builds and listings. The Pro plan at A$79/month gives you unlimited everything plus 80% revenue share.

What industries do you support?

+

We support 12+ industries including healthcare, finance, legal, real estate, education, construction and more.

How does the HR agent work?

+

Use our pre-built HR agents, start from templates, or describe your HR processes and we'll build a fully custom stack — covering hiring, onboarding, compliance and more.
